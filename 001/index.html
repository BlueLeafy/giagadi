Join Us on Our Organic Journey

Our 100% organic extra virgin olive oil stands out not only for its impeccable taste but also for its superior quality:

Flavor Profile

The taste is a harmonious blend of delicate and robust flavors, with a fruity aroma and a slight peppery finish, characteristic of high-quality olives grown in optimal conditions.

Rich in antioxidants, particularly polyphenols, our olive oil supports heart health, reduces inflammation, and promotes overall well-being. The presence of these natural compounds is a direct result of our organic farming practices.

Purity

Free from any chemical residues, our oil is as pure as it gets. The absence of synthetic substances ensures that the oil retains all its natural goodness, making it a healthier choice for you and your family.

Cold Pressing

We use traditional cold pressing methods to extract the oil, ensuring that it retains its nutritional properties and authentic flavor. This gentle process preserves the integrity of the oil, making it not only delicious but also highly beneficial.

Sustainability

By choosing our organic olive oil, you are supporting sustainable agriculture practices that protect the environment and promote biodiversity. Our commitment to organic farming ensures that we are contributing positively to the ecosystem.

Harvesting and Pressing

The olives are harvested directly from the tree, a method that ensures maximum freshness and quality. The pressing process is carried out cold, which is essential for preserving all the organoleptic properties of the oil. This method allows us to produce extra virgin olive oil (EVO) with an acidity level of 0.4, indicating very low acidity and high quality.
